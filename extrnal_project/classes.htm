Class Descriptions

Beginner Level

This level is perfect for anyone wanting to start yoga. NO EXPERIENCE NEEDED! We offer a welcoming and friendly environment with lots of direction. If you're new to the studio, but have experience, please feel free to come to a beginner class and then decide which level would be best for you from then on out.

Intermediate Level

Our intermediate level is designed for those wanting to elevate their skill. This level focuses on building strength, flexibility, and balance through a creative sequence of poses. Expect to explore more advanced postures, including arm balances, deeper backbends, and inversions, with options to modify based on your comfort level.

Advanced Level

Take your practice to the next level with our advanced level classes. Tailored for experienced practitioners seeking a deeper physical and mental challenge. This level featured complex sequences, advanced asanas, and transitions, including intricate arm balances, inversions, and deep backbends. With emphasis placed on strength, flexibilty, precision, and control.
